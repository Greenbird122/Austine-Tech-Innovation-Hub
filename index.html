— our story —

Three founders. Six live builds. One stubborn idea — that Bungoma can ship world-class products.

Austine Tech Innovation Hub was founded in 2026 with a vision to transform challenges into opportunities through technology. What began as a small team of passionate innovators has grown into a hub dedicated to developing practical solutions in agriculture, climate resilience, artificial intelligence, renewable energy, and digital transformation.

Innovation should not be limited by geography. Great ideas can emerge from any community, and world-class technology can be built anywhere. Guided by this belief, we design solutions that address real challenges faced by farmers, businesses, students, and communities across Africa. Our work combines creativity, research, and emerging technologies to develop impactful products that improve livelihoods, strengthen resilience, and create sustainable growth. From smart farming systems and climate technologies to intelligent hardware and digital platforms, every innovation is built with purpose and driven by impact.

Agriculture is where we started and where our deepest work lives. But we don't stop there. If the problem is real and the user is in Kenya, we'll figure out the tech.
