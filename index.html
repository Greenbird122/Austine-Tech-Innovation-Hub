— our story —

Three founders. Six live builds. One stubborn idea — that Kenya can ship world-class products.

Austine Tech Innovation Hub was founded in 2026 with a vision to transform challenges into opportunities through technology. What began as a small team of passionate innovators has grown into a hub dedicated to developing practical solutions in technology, climate resilience, artificial intelligence, renewable energy, and digital transformation.

Innovation should not be limited by geography. Great ideas can emerge from any community, and world-class technology can be built anywhere. Guided by this belief, we design solutions that address real challenges faced by businesses, students, and communities across Africa. Our work combines creativity, research, and emerging technologies to develop impactful products that improve livelihoods, strengthen resilience, and create sustainable growth. From smart technology systems and climate technologies to intelligent hardware and digital platforms, every innovation is built with purpose and driven by impact.

Technology is where we started and where our deepest work lives. But we don't stop there — if the problem is real and the user is in Africa, we'll figure out the tech.

— how we work —

From idea to live.

Every project follows the same disciplined pipeline. No shortcuts — just a proven process that ships.

  1. 01

    Discovery & Scope

    We sit with you — on a call, on WhatsApp, or in person — and map the real problem. No assumptions, no buzzwords. Just questions that uncover what actually needs building, who it's for, and what success looks like on Monday morning.

    Day 0–3 · Free consultation
  2. 02

    Design & Architecture

    Wireframes, component maps, data flow — we design the skeleton before writing a single line of code. You see mockups on Figma. You approve them. Then we architect the stack that fits your budget and timeline.

    Day 2–7 · Figma mockups shared
  3. 03

    Prototype & Iterate

    We build a working prototype — rough, functional, real. You test it with actual users. Feedback comes back. We iterate. This loop runs until the prototype feels right, not until the spec says it's done.

    Day 5–14 · Working prototype live
  4. 04

    Build & Polish

    The real build. Responsive, tested, production-ready. Accessibility baked in, performance tuned for Kenyan network conditions, dark mode, mobile-first — everything you'd expect from a studio that ships.

    Week 2–4 · Production build
  5. 05

    Ship & Support

    Deployed to your domain — Vercel, Netlify, or your own server. Handover docs included. We stay on standby for two weeks post-launch. If something breaks, we fix it. No extra invoices for launch-day jitters.

    Week 4+ · Live URL + 2-week support
